TOOLS BY ARCHETYPE
CHAOTIC CREATIVE
Essential Stack:

Voice Memos (Free) - Fastest idea capture
Evernote or Google Keep (Free-$10) - Organize chaos
Trello (Free) - Visual project boards
Freedom ($9/mo) - Block distractions during bursts

Why These: Low friction, visual, capture-first-organize-later, respect bursts

ANXIOUS PERFECTIONIST
Essential Stack:

Todoist (Free-$4) - Clean, reliable task lists
Freedom ($9/mo) - Eliminate perfectionism rabbit holes
Brain.fm ($10/mo) - Focus music to reduce anxiety
Notion (Free-$10) - Reference materials organized

Why These: Reduce overwhelm, limit options, clear structure, focus support

STRUCTURED ACHIEVER
Essential Stack:

Things 3 or Todoist ($50 or Free-$4) - Reliable task management
Toggl (Free-$10) - Track actual time spent
Habitify (Free-$5) - Build consistent habits
Fantastical or Google Calendar (Free-$5) - Schedule everything

Why These: Structure, consistency, measurement, proven systems

NOVELTY SEEKER
Essential Stack:

Trello (Free) - Visual variety
Forest App ($2) - Gamified focus
Notion (Free-$10) - Rotate project views
Evernote (Free-$10) - Capture new ideas

Why These: Visual stimulation, variety, gamification, multiple project juggling

STRATEGIC PLANNER
Essential Stack:

Notion or Asana (Free-$11) - Comprehensive planning

FLEXIBLE IMPROVISER
Essential Stack:

Voice Memos (Free) - Zero-friction capture
